Flowfield

Info:
   General info:
This project is by far one of my favorite, I just love it when music is synced up to something that happens.
It is just so sattisfying. In this project I used javax.sound.midi to read and play the midi files
I made this application mainly because I just really love music, and I wanted to make a small application
that combined music and programming. This program only comes with one midi file because I do not want to
be sued. You can find midi packs and songs all over the internet with simple google searched
