Purple rain is a visual application I made, I got inspired by The Coding Train: Coding Challenge #4.
The music used in this application is: Idealism - Controlla

In this application rain falls down endlessly with soothing music played in the background.
You can control the way the rain falls by moving your mouse from left to right.
