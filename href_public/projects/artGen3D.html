3D Art Generator

Info:
   General info:
I made 3D art gen because I was trying things out with the P3D renderer in processing, I was testing how
random coordinates would work in 3D and thought it looked pretty cool, so I decided to add
colors and lines between them, later I also decided to add some lights

   Version info:
Version: r1.0.0
This is a release version, and will probably not get any other updates

   Development info:
The development of Minesweeper Reloaded started at 1/5/2018, and also ended on the same date
I took a bit of inspiration from generative art, and the rest came with me just messing around
with the P3D Renderer
