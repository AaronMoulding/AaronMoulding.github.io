Temple Game

Info:
   General info:
In this game you control a character through a dungeon, but you can only move forward,
by landing on various tiles you will change your direction, or it will be your end...
What will be your highscore?
   Version info:
Version: r1.1.0
This is a release version, and will probably not get any other updates

   Development info:
This game was made for a school assigment where we had to make a game that's controlled
with just one button, in a week. This is what I and Joost Verbaarschot came up with, hope you enjoy!

   How to install:
First off all you will have to download the game. When it's downloaded you will have
to click on it on the drop down menu, now Android will ask you if you want to allow
installing unknown apps, accept this and go back to the installation, click on install
and after a few seconds of waiting it should be installed!
