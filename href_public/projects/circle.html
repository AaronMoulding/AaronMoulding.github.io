Circle Image Generator

Info:
   General info:
This was a small project of mine that I made because I got inspired by a video I was watching, where they had the same type of transition i made it this application.
This application also has a prefs.txt file, here you can change the image, background color, the amount of circles it makes at the start, the fps and
The speed the circles will grow. In this application the circles will grow until they touch another circle. Then they stop growing.

   Version info:
Version: r1.0.0
This is a release version, and will probably not get any other updates.

   Development info:
The development of this project started 28/4/2018, and also ended on the same day.
